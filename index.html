I am a Ph.D student studying under Chris Kapulkin at The University of Western Ontario. I study type theory and category theory, with a particular interest in the semantics of type theories, homotopy type theory, and higher categories. I'm intrigued by the realtionship between type theory and category theory and how we can use one to study the other.
